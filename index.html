Overwatch

Overwatch is a first person shooter where you and your team try to claim the objectives. There is both casual and competitive as well as arcade games. All are good fun.

In this game, you have a variety of heroes to choose from. There are three roles and each are important for victory.

Gamemodes

  • Assault
  • Escort
  • Control
  • Hybrid
  • Assault is where you either attack and defend the objective
  • Escort is where you stay near a vehicle and defend or attack the enemy team Your goal is to get the vehicle to the objective.
  • Control is where you take over a control point from the enemy and defend it until the time runs out.
  • Hybrid are a combination between assualt and escort.
